Garden of Salvation

Looking Glass

  • Approach the Portal
  • Defeat the Vex
  • Enter the Black Garden

Escape the Consecrated Mind

  • Split into 2 groups of 3
  • Team 1 opens barriers by shooting cubes and connecting tethers
  • Team 2 acquires Voltaic Overflow from the Mind
  • Combine at Field of Red, track Overflow, defeat Cyclopes and Overload Minotaurs
  • Mind teleports at the end, encounter complete

Undergrowth

  • Proceed from Field of Red into the Undergrowth
  • Jump across branches and mushrooms
  • Shoot bulbs to create platforms
  • Enter the Undergrowth Fortress

Undergrowth Fortress

  • Split into Runners (2) and Defenders (4)
  • Prevent Vex from sacrificing themselves at Fortress Relays
  • Kill Angelics to unlock cubes and connect to Relays
  • Acquire Enlightened Buff and kill Immune Vex
  • Repeat across 4 Relays, then all Guardians connect Cube to Middle Relay
  • Kill Immune Vex, Supplicants, Angelics; encounter ends after 2 min

The Consecrated Mind

  • Spawn Cube, connect to Middle Relay
  • Split fireteam: Team Gambit & Team Eyes
  • Team Eyes chases Mind, picks up Overflow, shoots Eyes
  • Detain Guardian calls Inner/Outer Eyes; Mind stunned, returns to Middle Relay
  • Team Gambit grabs motes and dunks into Overload Relay
  • Mind weak point exposed, damage Mind; encounter complete

Boundless Horizon

  • Proceed towards portal, take left to Boundless Lake
  • Jump on Vex Wheels and tree branches, shoot bulbs
  • Enter the Lake of the Sol Inherent

The Sanctified Mind, Disciple of the Witness

  • Shoot Sanctified to begin
  • Split fireteam into 3 groups of 2
  • Teams 1 & 2 enter Light/Dark portals, collect Motes, become Enlightened, kill Immune Vex
  • Team 3 stays to shoot weak points, kill Vex & Angelics, rebuild platforms
  • Team DPS cycles through portals and relays, dunking Motes and connecting Cubes
  • Destroy Sanctified Mind

Black Garden Pyramid

  • Proceed to center of Lake of the Sol Inherent
  • Pyramid top opens, Pyramid Scale hovers over lake
  • Jump into Pyramid, reach Pyramid Statue
  • Open end chests; encounter complete
