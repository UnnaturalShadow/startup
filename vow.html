Vow of the Disciple

Rhulk, Disciple of the Witness

  • Shoot Big Crystal for Leeching Force
  • Kill Glyphkeepers for symbols to appear (Leechers read Taken, Non-Leechers read Scorn)
  • Find matching symbols
  • Find the symbol on the Totems (L1, L2, L3 / R1, R2, R3)
  • Stand on plate with Leeching Force
  • Two Crystals spawn (Leechers shoot LEFT, Dunkers shoot RIGHT)
  • Leechers continue to share their buff
  • Dunkers with Leeching Force stand in Rhulk’s beams to gain Emanating Force
  • Once both Dunkers have Emanating Force, dunk at the Totem that was called out
  • Continue until the barrier reaches Rhulk’s Arena

Rhulk, Disciple of the Witness Arena

  • Enter Rhulk’s Arena
  • One person shoots Rhulk’s Glaive and gains Leeching Force
  • Everyone else calls out the symbol from the Glaive (Traveler (L1), Light (R1), Pyramid (L2), Darkness (R2))
  • Person with Leeching Force stands in Rhulk’s beams to gain Emanating Force
  • Person dunks Emanating Force into called-out Symbol
  • Rhulk’s armor glows, group shoots Rhulk’s weak spots (careful not to hit the Glaive)
  • Continue until all weak spots are gone, then DPS starts
  • When Rhulk becomes immune, run back to the raid banner
  • At FINAL STAND, use everything on Rhulk; if Pervading Darkness reaches 10x, the fireteam wipes

Exhibition

1st Room

  • Grab Resonant Laser
  • Kill Glyphkeepers (Taken/Scorn)
  • Kill Taken Knight with Resonant Laser for more Time
  • Relic Holders read Taken Symbols, everyone else reads Scorn Symbols
  • Find common symbol and shoot it on the doorway
  • Proceed to 2nd Room

2nd Room

  • Deposit Resonant Laser
  • Grab Resonant Laser & Vault of Glass Shield
  • Split into 2 groups of 3 (e.g., 3 Left, 3 Right)
  • Shield person cleanses teammates
  • Kill Taken Hobgoblins to spawn Glyphkeepers
  • Kill Glyphkeepers (Taken/Scorn) and Taken Knight
  • Read symbols, find common symbol
  • Repeat as needed, then shoot two symbols on the doorway
  • Proceed to 3rd Room

3rd Room

  • Kill Screebs
  • Deposit Resonant Laser & Vault of Glass Shield
  • Grab Resonant Laser, Shield & Eye of Riven
  • Split into 2 groups of 3
  • Shield person stays right, Eye of Riven cleanses blighted areas
  • Kill Taken Hobgoblins & Glyphkeepers, then Taken Knight
  • Read and find common symbols, move up stairs
  • Resonant Laser stays in middle to kill Taken Knights
  • Repeat until all steps complete, shoot doorway symbols, proceed to final room

Final Room

  • Kill Screebs
  • Deposit all items (Resonant Laser, Shield, Eye of Riven)
  • Grab same items again
  • Shield person stays in middle
  • Kill Taken Hobgoblins & Glyphkeepers, then Taken Knight
  • Read symbols, find common symbols
  • Jump to back of room, repeat steps
  • Shoot two symbols on the doorway
  • Proceed to Relic Room, kill Screebs, deposit items

The Caretaker

  • Split into 3 groups of 2 (2 Runners, 2 Stunners, 2 Defenders)
  • Defenders kill ads and shoot pyramid missiles
  • Stunners prevent Caretaker from reaching Obelisk
  • Runners grab symbols in the Dark Labyrinth and shoot them on Obelisk
  • Repeat until all 9 symbols are shot, then proceed to DPS

The Caretaker Attacks

  • Stand on plate on the side Caretaker walks toward
  • Three DPS plates: 1st (Heavy/Kinetics), 2nd (Kinetics), 3rd (Supers/Heavy)
  • Caretaker teleports upwards, stairs come down
  • After 3rd floor, Final Stand Room appears; use everything on Caretaker

Acquisition

  • Split group into 3 groups of 2 (3 Runners, 3 Defenders)
  • Runners kill Taken Knights and find symbols
  • Defenders protect Obelisks and read symbols from Totems
  • Runner/Defender pairs shoot symbols on Obelisk in order
  • Repeat until all rounds are complete

Disciple’s Bog

  • Kill Projection of Savathun
  • Enter Disciple’s Bog and arrive at Pyramid Barge
  • Stand next to Pyramid Barge to cleanse Pervading Darkness
  • Kill 3 Knowledge Bearers, collect Resonant Splinters, and deposit at Barge
  • Barge moves forward once all Splinters deposited
  • Repeat until Sunken Pyramid is reached, then enter Sunken Pyramid
