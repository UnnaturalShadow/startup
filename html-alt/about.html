    Welcome to RaidBuddy

    RaidBuddy provides guidance for Destiny 2 raids in the form of guides, images, and collaborative, interactive maps. This project is made possible with much thanks to SoteriaAugur who created the maps and provided the vast majority of the written content for the guides as well.
