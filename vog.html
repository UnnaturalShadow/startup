Vault of Glass Guide

Waking Ruins

  • Split fireteam into 3 groups of 2: 2 Left Plate, 2 Mid Plate, 2 Right Plate
  • Open the entrance to the Vault of Glass
  • Once at plate, stand in center until Vex Border is at max
  • Defend the plate from the Vex; don’t allow Minotaurs to touch plates or they’ll reset
  • Destroy Cyclops that spawn
  • Defend plates until the Vex Spire is completed
  • Vex Spire will open the door → Proceed to the Trial of Kabr

Trial of Kabr

  • Continue down the winding tunnels
  • Arrive at the Templar’s Well

The Confluxes

  • Split fireteam into 3 groups of 2: 2 Left Conflux, 2 Mid Conflux, 2 Right Conflux
  • Prevent the Vex from sacrificing on the Confluxes
  • Kill Vex that spawn in
  • Stay away from Vex Fanatics; if hit by their explosion, head to middle to be cleansed
  • Destroy Wyverns immediately as they spawn in
  • Overload Minotaurs will also spawn in place of Wyverns
  • Defend the Confluxes and prepare for the Oracles

Oracles

  • Split fireteam into Team Oracles and Team Defend
  • Team Oracles will listen for the correct order of Oracles and shoot them in order
  • Team Defend protects Team Oracle and clears enemies
  • 7 Oracles will spawn (3 Right, 1 Mid, 3 Left)
  • If order is shot incorrectly, fireteam needs to cleanse in middle at the same time
  • Once all Oracles are shot correctly, prepare for the Templar

The Templar

  • Assign 1 Guardian the role of The Aegis
  • Aegis uses the Super to break the Templar’s Immune Shield and cleanse team
  • DO NOT DROP THE AEGIS
  • Templar will try to Detain or Teleport
  • Aegis Guardian needs to block Teleport Circles
  • Oracles spawn in groups of 3; Team Oracle will shoot them correctly
  • DPS starts once Templar’s shield is broken
  • Destroy the Templar and proceed to the Gorgon’s Labyrinth

Gorgon’s Labyrinth

  • Fireteam lines up single file and avoids Gorgon gaze
  • If seen by Gorgons, team wipes
  • Proceed to center rock, then head left on rock wall until tunnel
  • Once out of the tunnel, jump toward Glass Throne entrance
  • All Guardians must be present to enter the Glass Throne

The Gatekeepers

  • Split fireteam into Team Plate and Team Time
  • Team Plate stands on Mars/Venus plates to keep portals open
  • Team Time enters Mars/Venus to kill Minotaurs and trade the Aegis
  • Shoot Vex, activate Portals, trade Aegis as needed
  • Once Conflux appears at Glass Throne, converge in center and prepare for Atheon

Atheon, Time’s Conflux

  • Destroy the Center Cube to begin encounter
  • Atheon spawns Harpies; leave 1 alive on left and right to limit spawns
  • Atheon randomly teleports 3 Guardians to Mars or Venus; Teleport Team handles Aegis and Gatekeepers
  • Oracles spawn in sequence; teleport team must shoot in order
  • After 3 rounds, begin DPS phase, cleanse Guardians as needed
  • Destroy Atheon; chests appear at Glass Throne
